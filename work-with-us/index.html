Our Standards

What We Look For

We work only with qualified professionals who take their craft seriously.

  • CIOL, ITI, or NRPSI membership (or equivalent recognised qualification)
  • Native or near-native proficiency in your language pair
  • Specialism in legal, medical, immigration, or business translation preferred
  • Professional Indemnity insurance (or willingness to obtain within 30 days of onboarding)
  • Legal right to work in the United Kingdom
