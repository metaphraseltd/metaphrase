Privacy Policy

Compliance, Confidentiality, and Professionalism

1. Introduction

Metaphrase Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

2. ICO Registration

As a professional translation agency, Metaphrase Ltd is fully registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). We adhere to the highest standards of data protection for our clients.

3. Data We Collect

We collect essential information to provide our linguistic services:

  • Contact details (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Source documents provided for translation, transcription, or interpretation.
  • Financial information for invoicing and payment processing.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide certified translation, accurate quotes, and project timelines, as well as complying with UK tax obligations.

5. Data Storage & Security

All digital files are stored using secure, encrypted cloud storage (256-bit SSL encryption). We implement strict access controls to ensure sensitive documents are only handled by authorized personnel.

6. Third-Party Sharing

Documents may be shared with vetted, third-party CIOL-certified linguists. All partners are bound by legally enforceable Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).

7. Data Retention

In accordance with UK business requirements, project files and financial records are retained for 6 years, after which they are securely erased.
