Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Metaphrase Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information and sensitive documents provided by our clients in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. ICO Registration

As a professional translation agency, Metaphrase Ltd is fully registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). We adhere to the highest standards of data minimization and confidentiality.

3. Data We Collect

  • Contact details (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Source documents provided for translation, transcription, or interpretation.
  • Financial information for invoicing and payment processing.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing certified linguistic services.
  • Providing accurate quotes and project timelines.
  • Complying with legal and tax obligations.

5. Data Storage & Security

All digital files are stored using secure, encrypted cloud storage (256-bit SSL encryption). We implement strict internal access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel handle your sensitive documents.

6. Third-Party Sharing

Where necessary, documents may be shared with vetted, third-party CIOL-certified linguists. All our partners are bound by legally enforceable Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) to ensure your data remains confidential.

7. Data Retention

In accordance with UK business requirements, we retain project files and financial records for a period of 6 years. After this period, or upon a verified request for deletion, data is securely and permanently erased.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request the erasure of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
