Frequently Asked Questions

How are your services priced?
Translations are typically priced on a per-word basis, and interpretation is priced per hour. We provide detailed, no-obligation quotes within a few hours of receiving your enquiry.
How do you ensure confidentiality?
As an ICO-registered agency, we follow strict data security protocols. All linguists sign legally binding NDAs, and all files are handled using encrypted systems.
What is your typical turnaround time?
Turnaround depends on complexity and volume. We always provide a firm delivery date with your quote. Urgent requests can often be accommodated — just let us know.
What does "CIOL Certified" mean?
It means our linguists hold the highest professional accreditation awarded by the Chartered Institute of Linguists (CIOL) — the UK's leading body for language professionals. Our translations are accepted by courts, the Home Office, and the NHS.
Do you offer remote interpretation?
Yes — we provide both in-person and remote (video/audio) interpretation for legal, medical, and business settings, available across the UK.
